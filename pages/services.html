Our Services

At our practice, we provide evidence-based nutrition care tailored to your individual health needs and lifestyle goals.

Chronic Disease Management

Nutrition plays a key role in preventing and managing chronic conditions. We offer personalised dietary guidance for:

  • Diabetes
  • Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
  • High Cholesterol
  • HIV & AIDS
  • Gout
  • Arthritis
  • Heart Disease
  • Lung Disease
  • Liver Disease

Digestive Health & Gastrointestinal Conditions

Specialised nutrition support to improve digestive health and manage gastrointestinal disorders, including:

  • Constipation
  • Diarrhoea
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)
  • Peptic Ulcers
  • Nausea and Vomiting
  • Poor Appetite
  • Food Allergies
  • Food Intolerances

Maternal, Infant & Child Nutrition

Supporting mothers and children through every stage of growth and development:

  • Preconception Nutrition
  • Pregnancy Nutrition
  • Postpartum Nutrition
  • Breastfeeding & Lactation Support
  • Infant Feeding Guidance
  • Complementary Feeding
  • Infant & Young Child Nutrition

Weight Management

Achieve your health goals with sustainable, practical nutrition strategies:

  • Weight Loss Programmes
  • Weight Gain Support
  • Obesity Management
  • Healthy Lifestyle Coaching
  • Behaviour Change Counselling

Sports & Performance Nutrition

Nutrition plans designed to optimise performance, recovery, and overall health:

  • Sports Nutrition Assessment
  • Performance Meal Planning
  • Recovery Nutrition
  • Body Composition Goals

Medical Nutrition Therapy

Individualised nutrition care for a wide range of medical conditions:

  • Anaemia Management
  • Tuberculosis (TB) Nutrition Support
  • Oncology Nutrition Support
  • Renal Nutrition Support
  • Hospital Inpatient Nutrition Care
  • Outpatient Nutrition Consultations

Specialised Dietary Planning

Personalised meal plans designed around your health conditions, preferences, and lifestyle:

  • Individualised Meal Plans
  • Vegetarian Diet Planning
  • Vegan Diet Planning
  • Mental Health
  • Therapeutic Diets
  • Family Nutrition Plans

Nutrition Counselling & Wellness

Empowering individuals and families to make informed nutrition choices:

  • One-on-One Nutrition Counselling
  • Workplace Wellness Programmes
  • Nutrition Education
  • Healthy Eating Workshops
  • Preventive Health Nutrition
