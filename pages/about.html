About me

Meet Esther Malebo Moyo

Esther Malebo Moyo is a Registered Dietitian with a Bachelor of Science in Dietetics from Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University. She is passionate about empowering individuals and families to achieve better health through practical, evidence-based nutrition care tailored to their unique needs and lifestyles.

With over five years of clinical experience in both the public and private healthcare sectors, Esther has worked across a wide range of medical disciplines, providing nutrition assessment, counselling, therapeutic meal planning, and medical nutrition therapy for both inpatients and outpatients. She currently serves as a Registered Dietitian at Pretoria West District Hospital while also leading her private practice, Elpo Dietitians, where she provides personalised nutrition services for weight management, diabetes, hypertension, gastrointestinal disorders, maternal and child nutrition, and overall wellness.

Throughout her career, Esther has collaborated with multidisciplinary healthcare teams, contributed to quality improvement initiatives, supervised service delivery, and participated in health promotion, patient education, and professional training. She has also served on the Mother Baby Friendly Initiative Committee and the Infection Prevention and Control Committee, reflecting her commitment to improving healthcare quality and patient outcomes. Esther remains dedicated to lifelong learning and has completed advanced training in Paediatric Nutrition, HIV care, Infection Prevention and Control, Strategic Planning and Management, and Public Service Ethics.

Esther believes that nutrition is more than just food—it is a powerful tool for preventing disease, managing chronic conditions, and enhancing quality of life. Her approach is compassionate, practical, and client-centred, focusing on sustainable lifestyle changes rather than restrictive diets. She is committed to equipping individuals and families with the knowledge, confidence, and support they need to make informed nutrition choices and achieve lasting health.

Whether you are managing a medical condition, planning for pregnancy, working towards your weight-loss goals, or simply striving for a healthier lifestyle, Esther Malebo Moyo is dedicated to partnering with you every step of your wellness journey.
