— How it works

Your Path to Better Health

Clear, practical steps from first contact to ongoing support.

  1. Book Your Appointment 📅 — Schedule an in‑person or virtual consultation at a convenient time.
  2. Complete Your Health Assessment 📝 — Fill a short health & lifestyle questionnaire before your appointment.
  3. Nutrition Assessment & Discussion 🔍 — We review your goals, medical history, current eating habits, activity and barriers.
  4. Personalised Nutrition Plan 🎯 — A plan tailored to your needs, preferences, culture, budget and lifestyle.
  5. Practical Guidance & Goal Setting 🍽️ — Meal planning, portion guidance, healthy eating tips, behaviour support and education.
  6. Ongoing Support & Follow‑Up 🤝 — Regular follow‑ups to monitor progress and adjust your plan.
